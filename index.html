What This Is
Not a guest lecture.
Not a webinar.

The Bespoke Show is a curated industry panel — 2 to 3 senior practitioners, moderated live by Sid Baliga, on a topic agreed with your institution in advance.

No motivational speeches. No recruitment pitches. Real practitioners talking about what actually works, what fails, and what they wish they knew earlier.

Students and faculty are the audience. The conversation is calibrated to a B-School campus context. The room leaves with something they can use.
